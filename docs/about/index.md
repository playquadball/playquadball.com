# About This Project

Welcome to the How to play Quadball Guide - a community-driven effort to make quadball accessible to everyone.

## Our Mission

To create the world's most accessible, accurate, and comprehensive introduction to quadball.

We believe that everyone should be able to learn quadball easily, regardless of their background or experience with sports. This guide serves players, officials, organizers, and curious newcomers alike.

## Core Principles

1. **Accessibility First** - Content written for complete beginners with clear, jargon-free explanations that anyone can understand.

2. **Educational Focus** - Prioritize practical understanding and real examples over memorization and abstract theory.

3. **Clear Guidance Over Edge Cases** - Focus on helping people understand common situations rather than getting lost in rare exceptions.

4. **Community-Driven** - Built by the quadball community with open contributions and responsive to actual user needs.

5. **Low Hierarchy and Efficient** - Quick decisions and action-oriented improvements without bureaucratic barriers.

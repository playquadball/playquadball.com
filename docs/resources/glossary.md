# Quadball Glossary

Essential terms and definitions for understanding quadball.

## General Terms

**Beat** - Successfully hitting an opponent with a live dodgeball, forcing them off stick

**Drive** - An offensive possession, from gaining the volleyball until losing it or scoring

**Flag Catch** - A seeker gaining sole possession of the flag, ending the game

**Off Stick** - State when a player cannot participate in play (must reset at hoops)

**On Stick** - State when a player can legally participate in play

**Reset** - (1) Going back to hoops after being off stick (2) Passing ball backward legally

**Seeker Floor** - First 20 minutes of game time when seekers cannot play

## Player Positions

**Beater** - Player who uses dodgeballs (black headband)

**Chaser** - Player who uses volleyball to score goals (white headband) 

**Flag Runner** - Neutral official who carries the flag

**Keeper** - Special chaser with zone powers (green headband)

**Seeker** - Player who catches the flag (yellow headband)

**Speaking Captain** - Designated team representative who can discuss calls with referees

## Equipment & Field

**Dodgeball** - Rubber ball used by beaters to knock players off stick

**Flag** - Tennis ball in sock attached to flag runner's shorts

**Hoops** - Goals of three heights (short, medium, tall)

**Keeper Zone** - Protected area extending from the endline to the keeper zone line (19m deep)

**Stick** - Required equipment held between legs during play

**Volleyball** - Ball used by chasers/keeper to score goals

## Game States

**Dead Ball** - Ball not in active play (e.g., after a goal)

**Live Ball** - Ball actively in play

**Possession** - Having control of a ball

**Turnover** - Change of ball possession between teams

## Penalties & Fouls

**Advantage** - Allowing play to continue after a foul benefits fouled team

**Back-to-Hoops** - Minor penalty requiring player to reset at hoops

**Blue Card** - 1-minute penalty box punishment

**Delay of Game** - Stalling or failing to advance play appropriately  

**Illegal Contact** - Physical contact that violates the rules

**Red Card** - Ejection from the game

**Yellow Card** - 1-minute penalty box punishment

## Special Rules & Concepts

**Dodgeball Immunity** - Keeper's protection from being beaten while in zone

**Gender Maximum** - Rule limiting same-gender players (4 max, 3 during seeker floor and in overtime)

**Immunity** - Beater's temporary protection while pursuing third dodgeball  

**Natural Motion** - Allowed completion of action when going off stick

**Possession Immunity** - Keeper's protection from tackles in zone

**Third Dodgeball** - Special state when teams each control one dodgeball

## Common Abbreviations

**AR** - Assistant Referee

**FR** - Flag Referee  

**HR** - Head Referee

**IQA** - International Quadball Association

**PB** - Penalty Box

**ED** - Event Director

## Timing Terms

**Game Time** - Official clock that stops during stoppages

**Overtime** - Extra play period if tied after flag catch

**Penalty Time** - Clock for penalty box duration

**Real Time** - Actual elapsed time including stoppages

## Scoring Terms

**Goal** - 10 points scored by getting volleyball through hoop

**Good Goal** - Confirmed legal goal

**No Goal** - Disallowed scoring attempt

**Target Score** - Points needed to win in overtime (loser's score + 30)

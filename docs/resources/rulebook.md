# Official Rulebook

This page provides access to the complete International Quadball Association (IQA) rulebook and related official documents.

## Current Rulebook

The official IQA Rulebook is the authoritative source for all quadball rules and regulations worldwide. This website's content is based on and derived from the official rulebook.

### Key Information
- **Current Version:** 2024 Edition
- **Governing Body:** International Quadball Association (IQA)
- **Scope:** International standard rules for competitive play
- **Language:** Available in multiple languages

### Accessing the Official Rulebook

For the most current and complete version of the official rulebook, visit:

**[IQA Sport Official Website](https://iqasport.org)**

The IQA website provides:
- Complete PDF rulebook download
- Rule clarifications and updates
- Official interpretations
- Tournament regulations
- Referee resources

## How This Website Relates to the Official Rules

This website provides:
- **Accessible summaries** of key rules for different audiences
- **Position-specific guides** extracted from the complete rulebook
- **Quick reference materials** for common situations
- **Educational content** to help newcomers understand the sport

**Important:** While this website strives for accuracy, the official IQA Rulebook is the final authority on all rules questions. When in doubt, always refer to the official source.

## Rule Categories Overview

The official rulebook is organized into these major sections:

### Section 1: Team Composition and Substitutions
Team rosters, positions, gender maximum rule, substitution procedures

### Section 2: Equipment and Dimensions  
Field specifications, hoop requirements, ball specifications, player equipment

### Section 3: Game Procedures
Starting play, stoppages, timing, ending games, forfeits and suspensions

### Section 4: Scoring
Goal scoring rules, restarts after goals, broken hoops, flag catch procedures

### Section 5: Dodgeballs and Being Off Stick
Beat mechanics, back-to-hoops procedure, natural motion, immunity rules

### Section 6: Physical Contact and Interactions
Legal contact, specific contact rules, seeker interactions, unsporting conduct

### Section 7: Boundaries and Balls
Ball usage rules, keeper zone, offensive/defensive play, pace of play

### Section 8: The Flag Runner
Flag runner role, requirements, timing, being ruled down

### Section 9: Penalties
Disciplinary sanctions, foul procedures, penalty box management, advantage

### Section 10: Game Officials
Referee roles, official calls, player-official interactions

### Appendix A: Definitions
Complete glossary of official terms and definitions

## Updates and Clarifications

The IQA periodically releases:
- **Rule updates** - Changes to existing rules
- **Clarifications** - Official interpretations of existing rules  
- **Errata** - Corrections to the published rulebook

Stay current by following IQA announcements and checking their website regularly.

## Regional Variations

While the IQA rulebook provides international standards, some regions may have:
- Local adaptations for youth or recreational play
- Tournament-specific modifications
- Equipment substitutions based on availability

Always check with your local league or tournament organizers for any variations from the standard IQA rules.
